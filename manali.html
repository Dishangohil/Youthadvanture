7-Day Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival at Manali, check-in, relax at Mall Road.
  2. Day 2: Local sightseeing: Hadimba Temple, Manu Temple, Club House.
  3. Day 3: Solang Valley for adventure sports (skiing, ropeway, zipline).
  4. Day 4: Rohtang Pass (subject to permit & weather).
  5. Day 5: Visit Naggar Castle, River Rafting near Kullu.
  6. Day 6: Optional paragliding at Dobhi, café hopping.
  7. Day 7: Departure after breakfast.
