6-Day Leh Ladakh Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrive in Leh, rest and acclimatize. Visit Leh Market.
  2. Day 2: Visit Shanti Stupa, Leh Palace, Hall of Fame.
  3. Day 3: Drive to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass.
  4. Day 4: Explore Diskit Monastery and sand dunes. Camel ride.
  5. Day 5: Drive to Pangong Lake. Overnight near the lake.
  6. Day 6: Return to Leh and depart.
